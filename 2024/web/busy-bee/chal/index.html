> Welcome to Busy Bee!

> Your objective is to write a bot that delivers honey from the flowers to the hive.

> Your bot can return 1 of 5 actions

   U - move up

   D - move down

   L - move left

   R - move right

   I - interact

> Navigate to the flower and have your worker bees interact with flowers to gain honey. Growing flowers (semi-transparent) reward 1 honey, fully grown flowers (after 7 ticks from spawning) reward 2 instead.

> Your worker bees can only hold 3 honey at a time. Any extra will be discarded.
